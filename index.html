FAQ

What is FTC?

Teams build robots to complete set tasks in regional, international and national competitions.

What do you do?

On the form below, submit your CAD files (STLs, assemblies etc) and receive a grant to buy parts!

Why?

FTC is expensive for most teenagers who participate and this serves as an opportunity to join and compete without financial burden.
